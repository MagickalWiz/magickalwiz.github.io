MagickalWiz's Senior Art Portfolio


Introduction

Hello! My name is MagickalWiz. I am known by other names, but I like to keep anything related to me off the internet. This is my Senior Art Exhibition portfolio, showcasing some of my work over the years.


Logo

I had ideas for a logo a couple years ago, but was presented an opportunity to create a professional-looking logo, compared to my rough, ugly one I had created for my Github main page. This logo was created in Illustrator.




Vector Portrait

This is a vector portrait created in Illustrator. I selected the family bird, Crispin, as the model. Sadly, he passed away last year. The portrait took me a long time to complete, as it was one of the first assignments and I wasn't used to the tools. I also had trouble at this point; over a couple weeks, I couldn't sign into Adobe. So I finally was able to start the project... the day after it was due. I'm not too happy with the shading on the hands, the fingernail in particular looks bad. At this point in the project, I just had to get it done because it was a week late and I was almost behind on other assignments.


Photo Composite

I created this image composite in Photoshop. It is a collection of screenshots from the game Destiny 2, in which I removed the backgrounds from the Guardians and resized them to fit the perspective. I took all the screenshots myself on a makeshift greenscreen. I did this be finding an area out of bounds that had a green sky which could be easily removed using Photoshop tools.


GIF

This is a GIF (I prefer the pronunciation 'jif') I created in Photoshop using the frame animation tool. I took screenshots of my friends in Destiny 2, and put them together along with some added effects. Thanks to Jordddd and Bontu for their assistance.


Digital Painting

This is my Guardian, digitally painted using Photoshop. I did not draw this freehand, I painted over the actual image of him (also a screenshot I personally took). I couldn't add a lot of details, so the belt and bandolier don't look great, but I'm otherwise proud of it. I will add a background sometime, as the black outline of Izanagi's Burden (the sniper rifle) is impossible to see, as is part of the gauntlet on his left arm.
